How Our Grading Works

Every card is evaluated by two independent graders and reconciled with weighted logic to eliminate curve bias. Subgrades are individually scored, logged, and cryptographically signed against your card ID hash before encapsulation.

  • 2× human graders + digital cross-check
  • failure tolerant custody chain
  • unique slab fingerprint + QR trace
